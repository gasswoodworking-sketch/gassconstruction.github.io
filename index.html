About Gass Construction

Gass Construction LLC is a family owned construction company based in Bergen County, New Jersey. For over 25 years we have specialized in custom woodworking and residential construction projects throughout the Tri‑State area.

Our focus is on quality craftsmanship, precision, and attention to detail. From custom kitchens and cabinetry to decks, trim work, libraries, gazebos, and outdoor spaces, we take pride in building projects that last.
